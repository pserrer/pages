Privacy Policy

Owsley's Odyssey · Last updated 4 July 2026

Short version: Owsley's Odyssey does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your game progress is saved only on your own device. There are no ads, no analytics, no accounts, and no third-party trackers.

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Owsley's Odyssey (the “App”, Android package com.pserrer.owsleysodyssey) handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App has no user accounts, no login, and does not ask for your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal information.

Data stored on your device

To make the game work, the App saves your progress locally on your device only — for example your coin total, hearts, unlocked power-ups, completed tasks, and sound/music settings. This data:

  • never leaves your device and is not sent to us or anyone else;
  • is removed when you uninstall the App.

Over-the-air updates

The App uses Expo's over-the-air update service (Expo Application Services, “EAS”) to deliver bug fixes and improvements. When the App checks for an update, the update service may process standard technical information — such as your device platform, operating-system version, the App's runtime version, and IP address — solely to determine and deliver the correct update. This is not used to identify you or build a profile. See Expo's privacy policy at https://expo.dev/privacy.

No advertising, analytics, or tracking

The App contains no advertising, no analytics or measurement SDKs, and no third-party tracking. We do not sell or share any data, because we do not collect any.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your choices

You can remove all locally-stored game data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage in your device settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at playstore@serrer.de.
